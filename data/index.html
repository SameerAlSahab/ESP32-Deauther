Authorised Use Only

This firmware is an educational security research tool for the ESP32 microcontroller. Read and understand the following before using any feature.

You Must Have Permission

You may only use this tool on networks and devices you own or have received explicit, written authorisation to audit. Testing networks without permission is illegal in most countries, including under the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA, US), the Computer Misuse Act (UK), the IT Act (India), and equivalent legislation worldwide.

What This Tool Can Do

Hydra-ESP can perform deauthentication attacks, handshake capture, PMKID capture, Evil Twin attacks, beacon spam, BLE advertising, and rogue AP cloning. All of these techniques can disrupt real wireless networks and constitute criminal offences if used without authorisation.

Responsible Use

  • Only test in isolated lab environments or on your own equipment.
  • Never target public, commercial, or residential networks.
  • Do not use captures (PCAP, HCCAPX, PMKID) to access accounts you do not own.
  • Store captured data securely and delete it when no longer needed.
  • Be aware that 802.11 deauthentication affects all nearby devices on the channel.

Liability

The authors and contributors of HydraESP accept no liability for misuse. You, the operator, are solely responsible for any harm, legal consequence, or damage arising from your use of this firmware.
